Reciepe Of Coffe

Ingrediants

  • Instant coffe -2 tbsp
  • Sugar -2 tbsp
  • Hot Water -2 tbsp
  • Milk - 170 ml
  • Ice Cubes

Procedure

  1. Take a bowl and drop instant coffe in it.
  2. Add sugar and hot water in it.
  3. Whisk the mixture now until you get a texture like a cream
  4. Take a glass and drop the ice cubes in it.
  5. pour the milk in the glass over it
  6. take a big spoonful of the coffe and drop it on the milk on top
  7. Give the coffe a little stir and it is ready to drink
